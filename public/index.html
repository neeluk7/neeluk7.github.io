Hi, I am a PhD candidate at EPFL, Switzerland, since fall 2021, working on confidential computing for RISC-V, advised by Prof. Edouard Bugnion and Prof. Thomas Bourgeat.

My primary research interests revolve around computer systems security, computer architecture, operating systems, and the RISC-V architecture.
